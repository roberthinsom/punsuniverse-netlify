What Are Puns and Why Do We Love Them?

Puns are a form of wordplay that uses similar-sounding or multiple-meaning words to create humor. But why are they so beloved?

  • Mental Gymnastics: They challenge your brain and provide a delightful workout.
  • Universal Humor: Puns appeal to all age groups, making them accessible and relatable.
  • Quick Wit: They make conversations lighthearted and fun.
